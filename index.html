Incredible New Modes

The Kinetic comes with 6 modes: 4 accelerometer and 2 classic. The two classic modes also allow you to create almost any mode imaginable, including almost any mode from other popular chips. Mode Control

Mode Control includes three settings: move to front, lock-in, and conjure. The Move to Front feature allows users to rearrange their modes into any order they choose. The Lock-In feature lets you choose which modes you want to show or hide. Conjure mode turns any one of your six modes into a on/off switch.
